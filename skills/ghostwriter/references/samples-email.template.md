# Email voice samples — template

This is a **template with fake examples**. Your real samples never live in this folder — installed skill folders get overwritten on update, and real messages are personal data that must not end up in a repo.

**Setup:**

1. Copy this file to `~/.claude/ghostwriter/samples-email.md`.
2. Replace the fake examples with 2–3 real emails **you wrote** — recent ones, in your everyday voice. Pick different relationships if you can (a coworker, a client, a stranger).
3. **Strip third-party PII.** The other person's name, email, company, and quoted text are *their* personal data — replace with placeholders like `<client>` or delete the quoted thread entirely. Your own habits are the signal, not the conversation.
4. Don't curate. Your typical email teaches more than your best one. Keep your real greetings, sign-offs, typos-you'd-actually-leave, and length.

**Format:** one `## Sample` section per email, with a one-line context note.

---

## Sample 1

*Context: reply to a close coworker about rescheduling.*

> can we push our 1:1 to thursday? wed got eaten by the platform review. same time works if you're free
>
> J

## Sample 2

*Context: status update to a client contact.*

> Hi <client>,
>
> The integration passed staging yesterday so we're on track for the Friday handover. One thing to flag: the export job runs nightly, not hourly like the old system. If that's a problem for your reporting let me know and we'll look at options.
>
> Best,
> Jamie
