# LinkedIn voice samples — template

This is a **template with fake examples**. Your real samples never live in this folder — installed skill folders get overwritten on update, and real messages are personal data that must not end up in a repo.

**Setup:**

1. Copy this file to `~/.claude/ghostwriter/samples-linkedin.md`.
2. Replace the fake examples with 1–2 real LinkedIn messages or comments **you wrote** — a recruiter reply, an outreach message, a comment on a post.
3. **Strip third-party PII** — the other person's name, company, and message get placeholders.
4. Keep your real register. LinkedIn voice is usually between email-formal and Slack-casual; the skill needs to see where *you* sit on that line.

**Format:** one `## Sample` section per message, with a one-line context note.

---

## Sample 1

*Context: replying to a recruiter I'm not interested in right now.*

> Hi <recruiter>, thanks for reaching out. I'm happy where I am right now so I'll pass, but the role sounds interesting — feel free to ping me again next year if it's still open.

## Sample 2

*Context: comment on a former colleague's promotion post.*

> Well deserved! Still telling people about that migration you pulled off in 2024. Congrats 🎉
